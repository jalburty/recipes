Tempeh, lettuce and tomato sandwich
Joe Alburty

This sandwitch recipe is similiar to the bacon, lettuce and tomato sandwich, but with the bacon replaced by tempeh.

Ingredients:
 tempeh
 tomato
 lettuce
 sharp cheddar cheese
 bread
 mayo
 cooking oil
 Optional:
  salsa
  sprouts
  avacado

1. fry slices of tempeh in oil until light brown
2. toast bread
3. stack sandwich in this order, bottom to top:
    slice of bread
    mayo
    salsa
    sprouts
    tomato slice
    lettuce
    tempeh
    slice of bread
