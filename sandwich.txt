Tempeh, lettuce and tomato sandwich
Joe Alburty
This sandwitch is similiar to the bacon lettuce and tomato sandwitch with the bacon replaces by tempeh.

Ingredients:
 tempeh
 tomato
 lettuce
 sharp cheddar cheese
 bread
 mayo
 olive oil
 Optional:
  salsa
  sprouts
  avacado

1. fry slices of tempeh in oil until light brown.
2. toast bread
3. stack sandwitch in this order:
    slice of bread
    mayo
    salsa
    sprouts
    tomato
    lettuce
    tempeh
    slice of bread
